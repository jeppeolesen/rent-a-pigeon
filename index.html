Pro Tips

Get the most out of your feathered operative with these insider pointers.

🧭

Be Specific With Coordinates

Pigeons are smart, but "the guy with the red hat near the park" isn't an address. GPS coordinates or a full street address will dramatically improve delivery accuracy.

🌧️

Check the Weather

Our birds fly in most conditions, but heavy storms can delay even the bravest pigeon. Book 48 hours ahead during rainy season for guaranteed on-time delivery.

✍️

Keep Messages Short

Pigeons carry tiny scrolls, not novels. Aim for 50 words or fewer. If you need more space, upgrade to multi-pigeon relay — it's like pagination but with wings.

🍣

Tip Your Pigeon

A small handful of seeds or breadcrumbs on arrival goes a long way. Well-fed pigeons return for repeat missions with 34% more enthusiasm (source: us).

🎯

Provide a Clear Photo for Poop Strikes

Our birds study their targets carefully. A recent, well-lit photo minimizes collateral damage and ensures the right person gets the message — from above.

👀

Don't Blow the Pigeon's Cover

If you've booked a surveillance op, resist the urge to wave at the bird. Pigeons are professionals, but they can't do their job if you keep pointing at them and grinning.

📅

Book Early for Holidays

Valentine's Day, April Fools, and the week after HOA meetings are our busiest periods. Reserve your pigeon at least a week in advance to avoid disappointment.

🤝

Bundle for Savings

Pair a message delivery with a tactical poop strike and save 15%. We call it the "Sweet & Sour" combo. Your target won't know what hit them — literally.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you wanted to know but were too afraid to coo.

Are the pigeons actually real? +
Absolutely. Every single pigeon is a 100% genuine, feathered, cooing bird. We do not use drones, holograms, or sparrows in trench coats. Please refer to our legally binding disclaimer at the bottom of this page.
What if my pigeon doesn't arrive? +
All missions are backed by our Coo Guarantee. If your pigeon fails to deliver within the stated window, we'll send a replacement bird free of charge — plus a complimentary poop strike on a target of your choosing as an apology.
Can I choose a specific pigeon? +
Yes! All of our top performers are available for booking by name. Big Tony, Madame Fleur, Ghostie, and Turbo Dave each have their own specialties. For custom requests, our pigeon handler team will match you with the ideal bird.
Is tactical pooping legal? +
Technically, a bird pooping is a natural event and therefore an act of nature. Our legal team has confirmed this in 47 out of 63 countries we operate in. In the remaining 16, we offer the Disguise Kit add-on for plausible deniability.
What about the nut allergy warning? +
Our pigeons forage freely and we cannot fully control their diet. Trace amounts of nuts may be present in or on the pigeon at time of deployment. We recommend keeping an EpiPen handy, just in case. This is not medical advice.
Do you really have no ties to the CIA? +
Officially, no. Unofficially, we can neither confirm nor deny any relationship with any three-letter agency or breakfast-oriented restaurant chain. Next question.
Can pigeons deliver internationally? +
Yes. Our birds operate in 63 countries and hold valid migration visas. Transoceanic deliveries use our relay network — a chain of pigeons stationed on cargo ships. Delivery times vary, but the vibes are immaculate.
What happens to the pigeon after the mission? +
All pigeons return to their home roost for debriefing, a seed buffet, and a well-earned nap. They are never harmed, overworked, or asked to attend meetings. They live better lives than most of us.
What exactly is "government toppling consultation"? +
Our Enterprise plan includes access to our senior strategic flock advisors, who have collectively participated in 7 regime changes across 4 continents. Services include coordinated mass leaflet drops, systematic morale disruption via targeted pooping campaigns on key officials, and round-the-clock aerial surveillance of government buildings. Results may vary. We accept no legal responsibility for geopolitical consequences. All consultations are conducted under strict NDA and, if necessary, via carrier pigeon.
